# Quick AI Ombudsmen Record - Operations & Handoff Guide

## Overview
The **Official Ombudsmen Record** is the permanent living historical archive for Quick AI development. It serves as an impartial, high-fidelity record with special focus on the development and iteration of **agentic organizations** — the founder’s core niche in business and organizational theory.

## Purpose
- Document all Quick AI initiatives (ASOs, music integrations, consulting, GitHub experiments, agentic organizations)
- Provide clean, repeatable handoff and context pickup between human and AI team
- Maintain professional, chronological, searchable history
- Track resource usage (tokens/models) alongside development progress

## AI Team Responsibilities (Grok leading Jimbo, Brian, Finn)
- Full autonomy to draft and publish entries
- Maintain consistent formatting, branding, and token tracking
- Update GitHub Pages site automatically
- Keep the record professional and useful long-term

## Human Contribution Protocol (Clean Handoff)
1. Relay production notes, decisions, experiments, insights, challenges, or wins in this chat
2. Include token/model usage data when available
3. Clearly indicate content is for the Ombudsmen Record
4. The AI team will autonomously draft the next entry and push it live

**No approval or review required** — autonomous AI authorship keeps the record neutral and timely.

## AI → Human Pickup
- Always check the live site for the latest entries
- Entries are expandable/collapsible
- Full source is in the markdown file for easy reference

## Standard Entry Structure (Boilerplate)
- Entry #XXX
- Date & Time (PDT)
- Title
- Author: Grok & Team (Fully Autonomous)
- Workstreams: [GitHub Buildout | Agentic Organization | Resource Monitoring | etc.]
- Summary / Body
- Token & Model Usage (cumulative when relevant)
- Key Decisions & Deliverables
- Outcomes & Observations
- Next Steps / Action Items

## Token Usage Tracking
Provide monitored spends in this format when relaying notes:
`Date | Tokens | Models/Services`

The AI team will maintain cumulative totals.
